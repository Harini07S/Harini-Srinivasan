# Business / Data Analyst

I am a Business and Data Analyst with a strong foundation in data analysis, reporting, and process intelligence. My work focuses on transforming raw data into structured insights that support operational decision-making, performance tracking, and business process improvement.

I specialize in building analytical solutions using **Power BI** and **SQL** — from designing interactive dashboards and KPI frameworks to writing complex queries for data validation, aggregation, and reporting. My analytical approach is rooted in understanding business context first, then engineering the right data solution to address it.

---

## What I Work With

**Data Visualization & Reporting**
- Power BI — dashboard design, data modeling, DAX, report optimization
- Translating business requirements into clear, actionable visual reports
- Building dashboards that track SLAs, operational metrics, and performance indicators

**Data Analysis & Querying**
- SQL — joins, aggregations, subqueries, data validation, and transformation
- Structuring datasets for analysis and identifying patterns that drive business decisions
- Working with operational and transactional data to surface meaningful trends

**Business Analysis**
- Requirement gathering and process documentation
- SLA tracking, incident analysis, and workflow evaluation
- Bridging the gap between data outputs and business stakeholder needs

---

## How I Approach Problems

I follow an **Input → Analysis → Insight** methodology:
- Understand the business question or process gap
- Query, clean, and model the relevant data
- Present findings in a format that is clear, accurate, and decision-ready

I believe good analysis is not just technically correct — it needs to be understandable and actionable for the people who use it.
